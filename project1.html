Lucidicium Finale



Iudicium Finale places you in the role of Saint Peter, the guardian of the gates of Heaven.
Your mission is to examine the life, choices, and secrets of each soul that stands before you, and decide their eternal fate.

Heaven or Hell.


This narrative-driven game was created during a game jam and focuses on moral dilemmas, judgment, and consequences.
Each decision impacts the final outcome, challenging the player to reflect on justice, mercy, and responsibility.
